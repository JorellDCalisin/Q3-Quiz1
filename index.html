Movie Reviews

Frozen

Synopsis: Determined to bring her sister back and restore summer to their kingdom, Anna embarks on a perilous journey accompanied by the rugged mountain man Kristoff, his loyal reindeer Sven, and the comical snowman Olaf, who dreams of experiencing summer. Along the way, they encounter mystical trolls, fierce wolves, and treacherous weather.

Director: Chris Buck Jennifer Lee

Cast: Kristen Bell, Idina Menzel, Josh Gad, Jonathan Groff

Reviews:

It was very adventorous and I loved it!

Wonder Woman 2017

Synopsis: Learning of the ongoing conflict raging in the outside world, Diana becomes convinced that she can help bring peace by defeating Ares, the god of war, whom she believes is responsible for the conflict. Despite the protests of her mother, Queen Hippolyta, Diana leaves Themyscira with Steve to venture into the human world.

Director: Patty Jenkins

Cast: Gal Gadot, Chris Pine, Connie Nielsen

Reviews:

It was very inspiring and emotional.

Teen Titans Go To The Movies

Synopsis: As the Titans face numerous challenges and setbacks, they must confront their own insecurities and fears. Through teamwork, perseverance, and a healthy dose of humor, they ultimately realize that being a hero isn't about fame or recognition, but about doing what's right and standing up for what you believe in.

Director: Aaron Horvath, Peter Rida Michail

Cast: Greg Cipes, Scott Menville, Hynden Walch, Khary Paytons

Reviews:

My family and I loved it and the action!
